Product

Train insane is a mobile application that will change the way trainers interact with their content.

Once logged in, trainers will be able to view a schedule of the trainer's schedule.

Trainers can plan and view sessions within the app. They are able to add exercises, reps and weight. Trainers can also edit weightloss progress as well as access emergency contacts

Older sessions will be archived and availible to send to clients for view.

With Train Insane, fitness professionals can manage training sessions, body composition and important documents all in one app!

Room for More- Next Steps

A client inteface for viewing will allow trainers to seamlessly share data with clients at any time.

In-app messaging will allow trainers to have more privacy. This service will allow for trainers to mass0 message members of group classes or send reminders and communicate with individual clients within app or from app to text message.

Conclusion

I enjoyed my time designing this project. As a former personal trainer, I was tempted to rely on my own experiences and assumptions to develop the app. In lieu of that, I stayed true to my UX knowledge and principles. When questions arose, I sought to answer them using surveys, interiews and user testing. After utilizing research and tools I had gathered, I was able to come up with solutions that were researched, tested and approved, thus making each new iteration better than the last.
